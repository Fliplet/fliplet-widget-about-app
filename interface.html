Help - variable reference

Variables will allow you to insert certain things into your app without having to type them or change them every time there’s a change. There are three available:

\{{appName}} - will insert the app’s name (as provided on Fliplet)
\{{organisationName}} - will insert your organisation name (as per the Fliplet system)
\{{currentYear}} - will insert the current year (very useful for the Copyright sentence)
